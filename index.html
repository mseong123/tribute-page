Elon Musk (2013 Time's Top 100 Most Influential People in the World) - commentary by Richard Branson

Here's a Timeline of Elon Musk's yowzer Life:

  • 28 June 1971 Born to engineer father and mother
  • age 3 Drifts off as if deaf(he was not)so parents get his adenoids removed
  • reads 10 hours a day
  • learns BASIC by finishing a 6-month course in 3 days
  • age 12 writes video game Blastar, sells it for $500
  • decides on his life mission - to save humanity
  • age 17 moves to Canada
  • transfers to Upenn double majoring in physics and economics
  • get Wharton economics degree(and later physics degree)
  • works 2 intern jobs:in ultracapacitor research and video games
  • leaves Stanford Phd after 2 days
  • 1995 starts Zip2 with brother, coding it himself
  • Gets $22million from sale of Zip2
  • May 1999 starts X.com, an online bank
  • 2000 merges with Confinity(competitor) to form Paypal
  • gets kicked out from PayPal(while on honeymoon)
  • learns how to build rockets from reading books
  • Jun 2002 Starts SpaceX with own money
  • son dies from SIDS (sudden infant death syndrome) 10 weeks later
  • gets $250m from sale of PayPal
  • April 2004 invest in Tesla
  • Mar 2006 first ever rocket built exploded
  • Mar 2007 2nd rocket fails
  • June 2008 Divorces Wife
  • Aug 2008 3rd launch fails destroying 4 satelites aboard including NASAs
  • SpaceX and Tesla running out of cash
  • Sep 2008 4th launch succeed and wins NASA contract for 12 flight for $1.6billion
  • Cries realizing SpaceX just barely escaped bankruptcy
  • Unveils first Tesla Model S
  • And the rest they say is history...Model X,SolarCity,Hyperloop,Model 3,Falcon 9
